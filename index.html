Our Features

An overview of what we provide

Launch calls by tapping on the contact photo and confirming the call

Swipe from the left edge towards the center of the screen to open the menu. Select contacts from the phone book and arrange them in the preferred display order.

Select a custom photo for each contact from the phone gallery or take a new photo with the camera. No photo? No problem! The contact name is displayed instead.

English, Spanish, German, Italian, Russian, Bulgarian, Chinese (simplified) and many more to come!

Technical Details

  • Outbound calls use the default calling app of the underlying platform
  • Tap & Call does not interfere with inbound calls
  • Only selected contacts (name, phone and contact photo) are imported from the phone-book
  • Custom images can be selected from the gallery or the camera
  • Phone-book contacts are never get modified or overwritten
